Fifteen Puzzle

Objective:

The objective of the Fifteen Puzzle Game is to rearrange the numbered tiles in ascending order to form the original image on the game board.

Instructions:

1. Click the "shuffle" button to begin the game.

2. Slide the tiles horizontally or vertically to move them around the empty space.

3. Your goal is to organize the tiles in numerical order to solve the puzzle.

Tips:

* Once you start playing the game, you cannot change the board size or the board image without resetting the board first

* You can slide multiple tiles simultaneously to expedite the rearrangement process.


Enjoy the challenge and have fun!
